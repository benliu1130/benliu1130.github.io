iPhone Game


Course:CSCI 526 - Advanced Mobile Devices and Game Consoles (University of Southern California)
Instructor:Mike Zyda
Project:iPhone Action Game
Language:Objective-C & cocos2d game engine

The game is free on App Store. Simply search "Q Monkey"!


Game control:
Tilt to move left and right. Tap to jump.

Goal:
Get as high as possible to get more score.

Other characters:
The player will die if hit a monster.
The player becomes a super man and fly if touch the pink monkey. The player earns bonus by hitting monsters when flying.
